Secret Diary" – Privacy Policy

Secret Diary" is a journaling app focused on personal privacy protection. All data is stored locally on your device only.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

This app runs entirely offline—no internet connection required. It contains no ads and does not request any sensitive permissions (such as location, contacts, or photo access). All diaries you write and any photos or videos you add are stored solely on your device and will never be uploaded to any server.

For privacy and security, all local data will be automatically and permanently deleted when you uninstall the app, with no possibility of recovery. Please be sure to back up important entries using the “Export as PDF” feature when needed.

You can use the app with peace of mind, enjoying a private, secure, and simple journaling experience.
