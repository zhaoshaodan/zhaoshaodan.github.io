Secret Diary – Technical Support

Thank you for using Secret Diary! This is a privacy-focused local journaling app. All content is stored only on your device and does not require an internet connection.

If you encounter any issues (e.g., forgotten password, PDF export failure, UI display problems) or have feature suggestions, please contact us via:

Email: support@zhaoshaodan.com

We typically respond within 48 hours.

⚠️ Reminder: Since this app does not include cloud backup, all data will be permanently deleted upon uninstallation. Please export important diary entries in advance.
